Our Story

A Legacy of Quality and Trust

Welcome to Sri Vari Textiles, Koothanallur's premier destination for exquisite clothing and premium fabrics. For the past 6 years, we have been weaving stories of tradition, style, and uncompromising quality into the lives of our valued customers.

Spanning across three expansive floors, our retail emporium offers an unparalleled shopping experience. From luxurious silk sarees for special occasions to comfortable everyday wear, we curate collections that celebrate both contemporary trends and timeless heritage.
